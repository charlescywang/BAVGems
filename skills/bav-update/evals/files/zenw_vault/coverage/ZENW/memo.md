# ZENW Investment Memo — 2026-06-01

*Post-model memo: results live here, never in the strategy report.*

## Thesis in one paragraph
Zenwave is a good business priced like a great one. The platform/hardware attach motion produces ~49% RNOA today, but every scenario in which that return structure survives intact requires the AI-telemetry attach flywheel (Bull) — and the market's $84 sits 19% *below* even our Bull value, i.e., the price embeds most of the Bull narrative. Weighted across scenarios the model says $52.42, a −37.6% gap: we would not own it here.

## What the market must believe at $84
Roughly: Platform ARR growth >22% sustained through FY2029, software mix >55% by FY2030, NOPAT margin ≥17.5%, and terminal returns held near 3× cost of equity — that is our Bull case with little probability haircut. Each scenario's story: Bear = white-box substitution (25% of ports by FY2029) plus AIOps displacement; Base = attach holds, margins ease to 15.5% as intensity normalizes; Bull = AI telemetry becomes a must-have and the expansion flywheel sustains NRR >120%.
